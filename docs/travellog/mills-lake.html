Exploring Colorado!

For about 4 days in July, I went with a couple of friends to Colorado. Over those four days, we did a lot of things, from hiking in the Rocky Mountains, to trying to climb the Manitou Incline, to just enjoying the good weather that Colorado had to offer.

Overall, the trip was greatly enjoyable, especially after a year and a half of limited outdoor time due to the pandemic. Although Covid regulations were still in place in Colorado, it was nice to enjoy the outdoors and have a little break from the Texas heat.
